What is Empório?


Empório is an endeavour to make the students aware of the practical aspects of business, commerce, and industry and to provide them with an enriching educational experience.The students will be able to explore and learn through the interactive, innovative and creative competitions. The competitions are planned to give the students a comprehensive exposure to the working environment, to enhance their thinking abilities, to stretch their imaginations, and to make them future winners.

Events

Chai Pe Charcha

“The aim of argument or of discussion, should not be victory but progress”

  • Participants 2/team
  • Criteria
    •Legitimacy of points/argument proposed
    • Debating skills
    • Diplomatic behavior

1. This event will be a group discussion with a qualifying round and the top teams will go into the final round.
2. Topic for both the rounds will be given on the spot.

Ven-You

“Planning is valuable though the plan is usually useless”

  • Participants 2/team

  • Criteria
    •Ratio of investments
    •Innovation/Out of the box thinking
    •Marketing strategy

1. Each school assigned a state of India.
2. Come up with a startup idea related to that state.
3. 4 minutes given to present the idea (with follow up questions)
4. Expected to come up with an investment requirement

Golmaal

“One cannot plan for the unexpected”

This is a surprise event where students will not only have a lot of fun, but will also stretch their imagination and knowledge to limits which tend to infinity.

Logo Story

“Your logo is an extension of your brand, and your brand is a story”

  • Participants 2/team

  • Criteria
    •Innovation/Originality of idea
    •Background research

1. Time: 3 minutes per team.
2. Participants given an industry upon registration.
3. Participants have to send a graphically designed logo before the event.
4. Required to present the story along with a tagline for the self designed brand on the final day of event.
